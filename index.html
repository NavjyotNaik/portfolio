UI / UX Projects

Easy Parking

Easy Parking is a UI/UX project focused on creating a smooth and intuitive parking experience for users. The solution allows users to quickly find nearby parking spaces, check real-time availability, compare prices, and reserve a spot in advance. The design prioritizes clarity, easy navigation, and minimal user effort, reducing the time and stress involved in parking. User flows were carefully structured to keep actions simple, while visual elements such as clear icons, readable typography, and consistent spacing improve usability. The overall experience is designed to be practical, accessible, and efficient, making parking management easier for both daily commuters and occasional users.

Calendar App for Kids

Calendar for Kids is a simple and easy-to-understand scheduling app designed to help children follow daily routines. The interface focuses on clarity, with clean layouts, minimal colors, and large readable elements to ensure information is easy to scan and understand. Activities such as school, homework, and events are organized in a structured way, reducing confusion and cognitive load. The design emphasizes straightforward navigation and consistency, helping children quickly understand what they need to do and when. By keeping interactions minimal and the layout predictable, the calendar supports better time awareness and routine management without overwhelming the user.

Parallax Experience

This project is a coffee shop landing page featuring a coffee cup placed on a table surrounded by coffee beans inside a café setting. The design focuses on strong visual hierarchy and a calm, inviting layout that highlights the product as the main focal point. Clean typography, balanced spacing, and warm tones are used to reflect the cozy atmosphere of a coffee shop. The landing experience is designed to be simple and immersive, guiding users' attention naturally to the coffee cup, brand message, and call-to-action without unnecessary distractions.

Web Development Projects

Automated Cyberbullying and Hatespeech Detection System

This project is a web application designed to detect harmful content and hate speech on Twitter across multiple data formats, including text, images, and audio. The system analyzes tweets and user-generated content to identify abusive language, hate speech, and harmful behavior, helping improve online safety on social media platforms. The application supports multimodal detection, where text is processed for abusive keywords and patterns, images are analyzed to identify offensive visual content, and audio inputs are examined to detect harmful speech. The web interface allows users or moderators to submit content, view detection results, and understand the severity of the identified content through a clear and structured dashboard.
